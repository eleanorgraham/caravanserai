DONATE

All charitable donations to Caravanserai Music should be directed to the Community Foundation of the Central Blue Ridge, which serves as our fiscal sponsor. If you are writing a check, please make it payable to the Community Foundation. Write Caravanserai Music in the Memo line of your check, which you may mail to the Community Foundation at P.O. Box 815, Staunton, VA 24402. You may also make an online donation through the Community Foundation at www.cfcbr.org. When asked how to apply your donation, please select Caravanserai Music from the drop-down menu. All donations made to the Community Foundation for the benefit of Caravanserai Music are tax deductible to the full extent of the law.
